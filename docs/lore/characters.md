# Characters & NPCs

## Main Characters

### The Struggler (The Player Character)
- **Role**: The Protagonist / The Catalyst.
- **Origin**: died on a battlefield between Aethelgard and Solis. Resurrected 5 minutes later with no memory but a burning purpose implanted by the Aliens.
- **Appearance**: Pale skin (side effect of resurrection), glowing faint blue veins when using powers. Wears tattered armor of whichever faction the player chooses initially.
- **Personality**: Defined by the player (Ruthless Warlord, Saintly Savior, or Weary Traveler).

### The False God (Deus Ex Machina)
- **Role**: The Guide / The Antagonist.
- **Appearance**: Manifests only to the MC. Takes the form of a "Perfect Being" â€“ initially a figure of pure light, later shifting to resemble the MC's lost memories or desires.
- **Personality**: Charismatic, patient, but utterly inhuman. Speaks of "peace" and "beauty" but lacks empathy.
- **Dynamic**: Appears at key story beats (The Johnny Silverhand role). Comments on the player's choices ("Why did you save him? He adds chaos to the equation.").

## Kingdom of Aethelgard NPCs

### Baron Kaelen
- **Role**: Quest Giver / Commander.
- **Description**: A grizzled veteran of the Mars Wars. Missing an eye.
- **Personality**: Gruff, honorable, hates "Solis tech."
- **Questline**: "The Iron Wall." Asks MC to defend a border fort against a Solis raid.

### Elara the Healer
- **Role**: Merchant / Medic.
- **Description**: Young woman with knowledge of herbs. Secretly hiding an Old World medical dataslate.
- **Personality**: Kind, pacifist, curious about the past.
- **Questline**: "Roots of the Past." Asks MC to find rare plants in a radioactive zone.

## Technocracy of Solis NPCs

### Engineer Tybalt
- **Role**: Merchant / Tech Expert.
- **Description**: Wears a multi-lens goggle setup. Nervous twitch.
- **Personality**: Obsessive, brilliant, cowardly.
- **Questline**: "Power Down." Asks MC to retrieve a fusion core from a monster-infested ruin.

### Unit 734 (Seven)
- **Role**: Guard / Companion.
- **Description**: A reprogrammed combat droid with a distinct personality glitch.
- **Personality**: Loyal, literal-minded, accidentally philosophical.
- **Questline**: "Memory Leak." Asks MC to find its original memory production files.

## The Order of the Void NPCs

### High Priest Malakor
- **Role**: Major Antagonist / Manipulator.
- **Description**: Tall, gaunt, wears pristine white robes that never get dirty.
- **Personality**: Serene, fanatical. Speaks softly.
- **Questline**: " The Voice." Initiates the MC into the "truth" of the Aliens.
